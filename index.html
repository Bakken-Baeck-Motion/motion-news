Worth a Deeper Look

Blender's node-based physics

Folding hair and cloth simulation into Geometry Nodes means the same procedural graph used for generative motion work can now also drive physical simulation — collapsing a line that's existed since node systems arrived in DCC tools. Combined with a 2-year LTS window, it's a credible signal that procedural/simulation pipelines will consolidate around node graphs rather than dedicated modifiers.

The makemepulse Codrops feature

It's a rare, granular look at how a top-tier interactive studio organizes cross-disciplinary work — web, XR, installation — around a single narrative asset and in-house engine. Useful less as news and more as a structural model for how a studio like B&B might frame its own multi-platform projects.
