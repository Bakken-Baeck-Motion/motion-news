Worth a Deeper Look

Lucy 2.5's live, real-time editing

Sub-40ms latency at 1080p/30fps means generative video is no longer just an offline rendering tool — it's fast enough to sit inside a live broadcast or interactive experience. For studios building real-time work, that's a genuinely new material to design with, not just a faster way to make a pre-rendered clip.
